A native Plex client for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and soon Apple TV. A fast, focused player for people who run their own Plex servers and want a client that gets out of the way.

Your libraries, front and center. No ads, no promoted content, no upsells.

PLAYS EVERYTHING
A hybrid playback engine handles virtually any format — MKV, HEVC, 4K, DTS, TrueHD, PGS subtitles, SRT, ASS, and more. All direct play, zero transcoding overhead on your server.

SUBTITLE & AUDIO CONTROL
Switch tracks on the fly during playback. Set preferred languages globally so Dusk auto-selects the right tracks every time. Supports forced-only subtitle mode.

SMART PLAYBACK
- Skip Intro & Credits with a single tap or auto-skip
- Continuous playback with auto-play next episode
- "Are you still watching?" passout protection
- Resume anywhere, synced through your Plex server
- Picture in Picture on iOS and iPadOS

BROWSE YOUR LIBRARY
- Continue Watching and Recently Added on the home screen
- Genre filtering and sorting
- Cross-library search with real-time results
- Full metadata: movies, shows, seasons, episodes, cast

MULTI-SERVER SUPPORT
Connect to multiple Plex servers and switch between them.

PRIVACY FIRST
Dusk collects nothing. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking. The app talks only to your Plex server.

OPEN SOURCE
Fully open-source under GPLv3. Inspect the code, build it yourself, contribute, or fork it.
