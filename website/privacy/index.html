Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

Dusk is a native Plex client for Apple platforms. Your privacy is fundamental to how the app is built. Dusk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. There are no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no third-party SDKs that phone home.

Data stored on your device

Dusk stores a small amount of data locally on your device to function. None of this data ever leaves your device or is sent to us.

  • Plex authentication token — stored securely in the iOS Keychain. Used to authenticate with your Plex server. You can remove it at any time by signing out.
  • Client identifier — a randomly generated UUID created on first launch. Used by Plex to identify your device as a player. It contains no personally identifiable information.
  • User preferences — your settings (subtitle language, audio language, playback preferences, appearance) are stored in UserDefaults on your device.
  • Image cache — poster artwork and backdrops fetched from your Plex server are cached locally for performance. This cache can be cleared by deleting the app.

Network communication

Dusk communicates exclusively with:

  • plex.tv — for authentication and server discovery. This is Plex's own service, governed by Plex's Privacy Policy.
  • Your Plex Media Server — to browse your library, stream media, and sync playback progress. This is your own server, on your own network or infrastructure.

Dusk does not communicate with any other servers, APIs, or services. There is no "home" for data to be sent to.

Analytics and tracking

None. Dusk contains no analytics frameworks, no crash reporting SDKs, no advertising identifiers, and no usage tracking of any kind. We have no idea how you use the app, and we prefer it that way.

Third-party code

Dusk uses one third-party library:

  • VLCKit — an open-source media playback framework (LGPL). It runs entirely on-device to decode video and audio. It does not collect or transmit any data.

Children's privacy

Dusk does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app requires a Plex account, which is managed entirely by Plex.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Since Dusk collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
